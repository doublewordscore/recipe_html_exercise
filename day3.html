Popeye's Green Shake

Description

This is a very healthy green shake with lots of vitamins, minerals, a little bit of protein.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of raw spinach
  • 1 large banana
  • 1/4 cup of almonds or peanuts (or 1 TBSP of peanut butter)
  • 1/4 cup of coconut water
  • ice

Directions
  1. Wash Spinach
  2. Peel banana and chop into smaller pieces
  3. Add spinach, banana, nuts, and coconuts water into the blender
  4. Add ice to taste
  5. Blend until smooth
  6. Enjoy!
